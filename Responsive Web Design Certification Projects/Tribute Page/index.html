Cristiano Ronaldo

Here's a time line of Cristiano Ronaldo's life:

  • 1985 - Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born in the São Pedro parish of Funchal, the capital of the Portuguese island of Madeira, and grew up in the nearby parish of Santo António. He is the fourth and youngest child of Maria Dolores dos Santos Viveiros da Aveiro, a cook, and José Dinis Aveiro, a municipal gardener and part-time kit man.

  • 1997 - Joined Sporting CP youth team and quickly climbed through the ranks.

  • 2002 - Promoted to the senior team the previous year whilst only 16 years old, impressing his peers hence making his professional debut on September 29 and scoring a brace on October 7 against Moirense .

  • 2003 - Signed by Manchester United after impressing during a friendly game whilst playing against Manchester United in Sporting colours.

  • 2007 - The year in which Ronaldo won his first league title under Manchester United colours helping the club win their 9th league title after falling short for the past 4 years.

  • 2008 - Playing in his first champions league final hosted in Moscow against Chelsea. Cristiano scored a headed goal in the first half and continued to pose a threat through out the entire match in turn winning his first Champions league title.

  • 2009 - Won his first Balon d'or after the heroics in the Champions League final the previous year. Also earining him atransfer in the summer window switching clubs moving to Real Madrid with the Spanish giants breaking the transfer fee at the time in order to acquire his signature.

  • 2013 - Won his first La liga title with Real Madrid under the guidance of a fellow country man, the legendary Jose Mourinho.

  • 2018 - Winning his 5th Champions League title with Real Madrid, also moving to the Seria A giants Juventus.
